Introduction

Description

This piece of research explores the possibility of authenticating a subject on the basis of his very specific biometric identity - gait i.e. way of walking. It probes the Machine Learning paradigm to achieve the objectives with implementing model based on modification of a general purpose algorithm. The goal for the research work constitutes to establish the Gait Based Authentication System (GBAS) as a reliable and alternate means of authentication in the biometric domain. Further a Proof of Concept is also developed to showcase the studied observations.
